Santanu Pal

I am a lead scientist at Wipro AI Lab (Lab45). My area of research includes: Natural Language Processing, Social AI, Machine Translation (Neural, Statistical), Automatic Post-Editing, Multi-Word Expressions, Question Answering and Question Generation, Human Computer Interaction, Multi-modal Interface, Multi-modal Machine Translation, Speech Translation.

Santanu Pal (PhD)

Lead Scientist at Wipro AI Lab (Lab45)

I did my post-doc at Saarland University. During my postdoc I worked on Multi-modal Post-editing (MMPE) of the EU-DFG project in Saarland University, Germany.

I completed my PhD from Saarland University in Nov, 2017. The PhD supported by EU project EXPloiting Empirical appRoaches to Translation (EXPERT) sponsored by Marie Curie.

Before my PhD, I did my Masters in Computer Technology from Jadavpur University, and also served as a Research Engineer in Jadavpur University on project " English to Indian Language Machine translation (EILMT) Phase I & Phase II ", Ministry of Communications & Information Technology, Government of India.
