About Me

Core Expertise

I am a **Computational Linguist and AI Researcher** specializing in **Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Translation (MT), and Generative AI**. My research focuses on developing **multilingual, human-centric AI systems** for healthcare, legal, and tourism.

Research Vision

My ultimate goal is to create **adaptive, trustworthy AI** that enhances global communication, improves decision-making, and drives long-term positive behavioral change. I champion habit formation models, multilingual AI, and responsible generative NLP.

Professional Journey

With over a decade in academic and industry R&D, I began my NLP journey in 2008 at Jadavpur University. My PhD and postdoc at Saarland University focused on MT. Since 2020, as a Senior Architect and Scientist at Wipro, I've led projects, collaborated academically, and secured a **US patent**. For more details about my work, explore my comprehensive publications.

Community Engagement

I actively contribute to the MT research community by organizing **WMT workshops and shared tasks**. My work is published in **top-tier conferences and journals**, and I serve as a reviewer, Program Committee (PC), Senior Program Committee (SPC), and Area Chair (AC) member for leading venues.
