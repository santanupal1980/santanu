I am a lead scientist at Wipro AI Lab (Lab45).

Post-doc from Saarland University, Germany. During my postdoc I worked on Multi-modal Post-editing (MMPE) - A EU-DFG project

PhD from Saarland University, Germany (completed in Nov, 2017). The PhD supported by EU project EXPloiting Empirical appRoaches to Translation (EXPERT) sponsored by Marie Curie.
